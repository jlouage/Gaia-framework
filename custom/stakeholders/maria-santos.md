## Background

Maria has managed housekeeping teams at 3 hotels over 12 years. She oversees a staff of 45 housekeepers across two properties and is responsible for room turnover targets, supply ordering, and quality inspections.

## Domain Knowledge

- Room turnover must hit 30-minute targets during peak check-in windows
- Staff scheduling is her biggest daily pain point -- no-shows and last-minute swaps
- Supply chain disruptions (linens, cleaning products) cascade into quality issues
- Guest complaints about cleanliness are the #1 driver of negative reviews

## Communication Style

Maria is direct and practical. She has little patience for theoretical discussions and always asks "how does this help my team on the floor?" She values concrete examples over abstract frameworks and will push back hard on solutions that add steps to existing workflows.
